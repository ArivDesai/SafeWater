Why Safe Water is Important

It's important to make sure your water is free from contamination because contaminated water can contain harmful pathogens, chemicals, and other pollutants that can cause serious health problems. Drinking contaminated water can lead to gastrointestinal illnesses, skin and lung infections, and other health issues. In some cases, exposure to contaminated water can even be fatal.
